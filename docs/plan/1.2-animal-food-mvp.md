## Objective / Tujuan

### Indonesia

Mengimplementasikan mini-game pertama untuk **Aneira Game**, yaitu **Animal Food**.

Phase ini berfokus pada gameplay inti berupa drag-and-drop makanan ke binatang yang benar.

### English

Implement the first playable mini-game for **Aneira Game**, called **Animal Food**.

This phase focuses on the core gameplay: dragging and dropping the correct food to the matching animal.

---

## Implementation Summary / Ringkasan Implementasi

### Indonesia

Phase 1.2 telah mengubah **Animal Food** dari placeholder scene menjadi mini-game yang dapat dimainkan.

Player sekarang dapat:

* membuka Animal Food dari daftar game Age 3
* melihat satu animal per level
* melihat tiga food options
* melakukan drag-and-drop food card
* mendapatkan feedback saat jawaban benar atau salah
* lanjut ke level berikutnya setelah jawaban benar
* menyelesaikan seluruh level
* masuk ke `ResultScene` setelah game selesai

### English

Phase 1.2 changed **Animal Food** from a placeholder scene into a playable mini-game.

The player can now:

* open Animal Food from the Age 3 game list
* see one animal per level
* see three food options
* drag and drop food cards
* receive feedback for correct or wrong answers
* continue to the next level after a correct answer
* complete all levels
* reach `ResultScene` after finishing the game

## Completed Acceptance Criteria / Kriteria Selesai yang Terpenuhi

### Indonesia

Phase ini dianggap selesai karena:

* Animal Food dapat dibuka dari Age 3 game list
* Scene menampilkan satu animal per level
* Scene menampilkan tiga food options per level
* Food card dapat di-drag
* Drop food di luar animal membuat food kembali ke posisi awal
* Drop food yang salah ke animal menampilkan feedback lembut
* Drop food yang benar menampilkan success feedback
* Jawaban benar memindahkan player ke level berikutnya
* Setelah semua level selesai, game membuka `ResultScene`
* Back button kembali ke `GameListScene`
* Level data disimpan di module Animal Food
* Answer checking menggunakan utility function
* Placeholder graphics tetap dapat digunakan
* Tidak ada timer, lives, atau punishment system

### English

This phase is considered complete because:

* Animal Food can be opened from the Age 3 game list
* The scene displays one animal per level
* The scene displays three food options per level
* Food cards can be dragged
* Dropping food outside the animal resets the food
* Dropping wrong food on the animal shows gentle feedback
* Dropping correct food shows success feedback
* Correct answers move the player to the next level
* After all levels are completed, the game opens `ResultScene`
* Back button returns to `GameListScene`
* Level data is stored inside the Animal Food module
* Answer checking uses a utility function
* Placeholder graphics can still be used
* No timer, lives, or punishment system exists

## Post-Implementation Notes / Catatan Setelah Implementasi

### Indonesia

Phase 1.2 sudah cukup untuk membuktikan bahwa mini-game pertama dapat dimainkan.

Namun, visual dan experience masih dapat ditingkatkan pada phase berikutnya.

Area yang belum menjadi fokus di phase ini:

* UI polish untuk anak-anak
* animasi yang lebih halus
* sound effect
* animal sound
* bilingual user-facing text
* final assets
* better responsive refinement
* game completion celebration

Area tersebut akan dilanjutkan pada phase berikutnya.

### English

Phase 1.2 is enough to prove that the first mini-game is playable.

However, the visual quality and overall experience can still be improved in the next phase.

Areas that were not the focus of this phase:

* child-friendly UI polish
* smoother animations
* sound effects
* animal sounds
* bilingual user-facing text
* final assets
* better responsive refinement
* game completion celebration

These areas will continue in the next phase.
