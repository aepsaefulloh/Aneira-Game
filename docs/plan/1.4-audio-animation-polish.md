### Indonesia

Menambahkan polish audio dan animasi ringan pada **Animal Food** agar pengalaman bermain terasa lebih hidup, menyenangkan, dan tetap ramah anak.

### English

Add light audio and animation polish to **Animal Food** so the play experience feels more alive, delightful, and still child-friendly.

## Audio Requirements / Kebutuhan Audio

### Indonesia

Tambahkan audio ringan:

```txt
correct answer  → soft success sound
wrong answer    → gentle retry sound
button tap      → soft tap sound
animal sound    → optional placeholder
```

Rules:

* audio harus bisa dimatikan
* mute preference disimpan di localStorage
* missing audio tidak boleh membuat game crash
* jangan gunakan sound yang keras, mengejutkan, atau agresif

### English

Add light audio:

```txt
correct answer  → soft success sound
wrong answer    → gentle retry sound
button tap      → soft tap sound
animal sound    → optional placeholder
```

Rules:

* audio must be mutable
* mute preference is saved in localStorage
* missing audio must not crash the game
* do not use loud, startling, or aggressive sounds

---

## AudioManager / Manager Audio

### Indonesia

Buat `AudioManager` sederhana untuk mengelola:

* play sound by key
* mute/unmute
* get mute status
* save mute preference
* safe play jika sound key tidak tersedia

### English

Create a simple `AudioManager` to manage:

* play sound by key
* mute/unmute
* get mute status
* save mute preference
* safe play if a sound key is unavailable

Suggested API:

```ts
AudioManager.play(scene, 'animal-food-success')
AudioManager.setMuted(true)
AudioManager.toggleMuted()
AudioManager.isMuted()
```
